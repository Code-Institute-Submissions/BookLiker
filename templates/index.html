Welcome to the BookLiker app

This is a self-maintained book collection app. You can sort or filter the books from the Sort by dropdown or just check out the collection in the order the books have been added to the collection below.

With the Add New Book option you can add book to the collection, please follow the instructions carefully

To check the book's price on an online shop, please click on the Buy online button

For more info, or if you would like to edit a book, just click on the more info button on the book card

If you would like to express that you like/dislike a book, just click on the icons on the book card
